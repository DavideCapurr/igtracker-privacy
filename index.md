# Privacy Policy — IGTracker

**Last updated:** 2026-04-20

## 1. Overview

IGTracker ("the App") is a utility that helps users analyze changes in their social network followers/following lists. This policy describes what data the App processes and how.

## 2. Data Controller

- **Developer:** la.spa
- **Contact:** [la.meedeea@gmail.com]

## 3. Data We Process

The App processes the following categories of data, **exclusively on your device**:

| Data | Source | Storage | Purpose |
|------|--------|---------|---------|
| Session cookies | Login via in-app browser | WKWebView local store | Authenticating requests to display your own lists |
| Username, profile picture URL | Social network public profile | Local UserDefaults | Display in the app interface |
| Follower / Following usernames | Your account's own lists | Local SwiftData database | Compare snapshots to detect changes |
| Subscription status | Apple StoreKit | Apple servers (managed by Apple) | Unlock Pro features |

## 4. Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do **NOT** send your credentials, cookies, or follower lists to our servers.
- We do **NOT** have servers that store personal data.
- We do **NOT** track your activity across apps or websites.
- We do **NOT** share data with third parties for advertising.
- We do **NOT** use analytics SDKs.

## 5. Third-Party Services

- **Apple StoreKit**: subscription payments are processed by Apple. See [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).
- **Social network (login)**: when you log in through the in-app browser, your session is established directly with the social network. Consult their privacy policy for how they process your login.

## 6. Data Retention

All App data is stored locally on your device. You can delete it at any time through:

**Settings → Pro → Privacy & Data → Delete all data**

This removes: follower history, login session, local preferences. The subscription itself must be canceled separately through the iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

Uninstalling the App also permanently deletes all local data.

## 7. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Because all data is processed on your device and not by us, you have direct and complete control over it. You can:

- **Access**: view your data in the App's lists
- **Delete**: use the "Delete all data" button in Settings
- **Port**: no portable format is provided (the data is your own social network data, available directly from the source)

## 8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly process data from children.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated in-app or via a new version.

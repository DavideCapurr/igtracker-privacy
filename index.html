Privacy Policy — IGTracker

Last updated: 2026-04-20

1. Overview

IGTracker ("the App") is a utility that helps users analyze changes in their social network followers/following lists. This policy describes what data the App processes and how.

2. Data Controller

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT send credentials, cookies, or follower lists to our servers.
  • We do NOT operate servers that store personal data.
  • We do NOT track activity across apps or websites.
  • We do NOT share data with third parties for advertising.
  • We do NOT use analytics SDKs.

5. Third-Party Services

Apple StoreKit: subscription payments are processed by Apple. See Apple's Privacy Policy.
Social network (login): login is established directly with the social network via the in-app browser. Consult their privacy policy for details.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. Delete it at any time via:
Settings → Pro → Privacy & Data → Delete all data
Uninstalling the App also permanently deletes all local data. Subscriptions must be canceled separately via iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

7. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Because all data is on your device, you have direct control: Access data in the App's lists; Delete via the settings button above.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly process data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

Material changes will be communicated in-app or via a new App version.
